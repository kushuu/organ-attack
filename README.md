# Organ Attack

Organ Attack is a card game where players compete to eliminate their opponents by playing cards from their hand to attack their organs.

## How to Play

1. Each player is dealt a hand of cards.
2. Players take turns playing cards from their hand to attack their opponents' organs.
3. The game continues until one player eliminates all of their opponents.

## Game Rules

1. Each player has a set of organs that they must protect from being eliminated.
2. Players can play cards from their hand to attack their opponents' organs.
3. The game continues until one player eliminates all of their opponents.

## Game Objective

The objective of the game is to eliminate all of your opponents by playing cards from your hand to attack their organs.

## Game Variants

1. Single Player: Play against the computer.
2. Multiplayer: Play against multiple players.
